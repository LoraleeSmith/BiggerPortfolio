About Me

In the mean time of finding out what I want to do I got an Associate's degree in Social Behavior and another in General Buisness Management. I really wanted to work from home and I had messed around with creating a website and blog on Wix.com but learned that I didn't know how to really set it up how I wanted and was envisioning. Thus, I enrolled in UCDavis Web Development coding bootcamp. Through this course I am learning the techniques and proper coding and systems to make my creative visions come to life. I hope to help others bring their dream come to life as well. I enjoy working with sytlists to create beautiful web portfolios of their work, stunning web portfolios for my photograpghy friends to display their work and much more.
